Does your garden need an overhaul?

Affordable Scapes offer a broad range of quality professional services. Our focus is on low maintenance, drought tolerant sustainable gardens. It is our aim to provide you with a garden that will continue to grow and develop long after our work has completed.

The bulk of our work is in the Western and Northern suburbs, but we readily travel right across Melbourne. In fact there isn't anywhere in Victoria that we have said no to yet!
